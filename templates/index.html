How to use the Map

You can navigate through the map via your cursor and click and dragging the cursor around the map.

You can see the area in which is occupied with green land via a look through the map on the highlighted areas in green.

Who are we?

Our website aims to inform you about the green land within the urban areas of England and consist of analysis of different lands percentage (that consists of green land) and the comparison between them.

Sustainability Goals

SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities and Communities):

Directly measures and visualizes progress toward Target 11.7 (universal access to green space) and provides data for resilient city planning.


SDG 3 (Good Health and Well-being):

Provides the data foundation for policy interventions that improve mental and physical health by increasing accessible natural environments.


SDG 15 (Life on Land):

Supports the preservation and creation of urban ecosystems and encourages biodiversity within cities.
